My First Post

Hello and welcome to my personal blog! I’m Ammarah, a Computer Science student on a spiritual quest, with a heart set on building a deeper connection with my Lord and Creator. I invite you to join me as I walk this path — let’s explore, learn, and draw closer to our Creator together!
